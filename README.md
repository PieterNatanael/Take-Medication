Privacy Policy


Take Medication
Last updated: April 2026

Overview
Take Medication ("the App") is a simple tool designed to help users track and manage their medication schedules.
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection
We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.
No user accounts
No analytics or tracking
No third-party SDKs that collect data
No data is sent to external servers
All information you enter into the App remains on your device.
Local Data Storage
All data, including:
medication schedules
notes
history and timestamps
is stored locally on your device only.
The Developer does not have access to your data.
Data Responsibility and Risk
You are responsible for your own data.
While the App is designed to help track medication, we do not guarantee data persistence or accuracy.
Data may be lost due to:
app updates
device issues
software bugs or crashes
uninstalling the App
You should not rely on the App as your sole record for important medical or health-related information.
Device Permissions
The App may use device features such as:
notifications (for reminders)
These are used only to provide core functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.
Medical Disclaimer
Take Medication is not a medical or healthcare application.
The App does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions
Do not rely on the App as a substitute for professional medical advice
Subscriptions and Purchases
The App may offer:
auto-renewable subscriptions (e.g. yearly plans)
one-time purchases (lifetime access)
All payments are processed by the platform provider (such as Apple App Store or Google Play).
The Developer does not have access to your payment information.
Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13, as no data is collected at all.
Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated "Last updated" date.
